Why TechCare

From scattered records to one reliable workflow.

Manual inventory logs make availability unclear, approvals slow, and errors easy to miss. TechCare turns that fragmented process into a focused mobile experience for both students and admins.

01

The problem

Manual tracking creates blind spots.

Paper forms and disconnected records make it difficult to know what is available, who has an item, and which request needs action.

02

The solution

A digital system everyone can follow.

TechCare connects inventory, rental requests, approval decisions, stock levels, and member access in one clear workflow.

What it does

Every essential workflow.
Right in your pocket.

Purpose-built tools give admins control while keeping rental requests simple and transparent for students.
